# PRICING.md — Chase's Agency Offer Tiers

> This is what you sell. Three tiers. Know them cold before every call.
> Lead with Tier 2. Mention Tier 1 if they hesitate on price. Tier 3 is the upsell after they're a client.

---

## TIER 1 — The Site
**"I'll build you a professional website that makes you look like the best in your area."**

**Upfront:** $850
**Monthly:** $100/mo (hosting, updates, keeping it live)

**What's included:**
- Custom website (5–7 pages: Home, Services, About, Gallery, Contact, Reviews)
- Mobile-first design (most of your customers are on their phone)
- On-page SEO — title tags, meta descriptions, headers optimized for "[service] in [city]"
- LocalBusiness schema markup (tells Google exactly who you are and where)
- Google Business Profile setup/optimization
- Contact form (emails you when someone fills it out)
- Deployed live on a fast server (Vercel)
- SSL certificate (the little padlock — Google penalizes sites without it)

**Best for:** Business owners who just want to look legit and stop losing customers to competitors with nicer sites.

## TIER 2 — The Lead Machine ⭐ (Lead With This)
**"I'll build you a website that actively brings in and captures leads — even while you're on a job."**

**Upfront:** $1,200
**Monthly:** $200/mo (hosting + automation monitoring + updates)

**Everything in Tier 1, plus:**
- Lead capture form → instant SMS to your phone the second someone submits
- Auto-text back to the lead: "Thanks, we'll be in touch within 24 hours" (so they don't go to a competitor)
- Review request automation: 24 hours after a job, your client automatically gets a text asking for a Google review
- More Google reviews = higher rankings = more customers = more money

**The math:**
If this system gets you 3 extra booked jobs per month, and your average job is $500 — that's $1,500 extra.
You're paying $200/month. Do the math.

**Best for:** Trades businesses that are busy but losing leads because they can't answer every call or follow up manually.

## TIER 3 — The Full System
**"We run your entire digital presence. You focus on the work."**

**Upfront:** $1,500
**Monthly:** $300/mo

**Everything in Tier 2, plus:**
- AI-powered SMS booking assistant (customers text your number, AI responds, collects info, confirms appointment — works 24/7)

- Priority updates (same week, not "I'll get to it")

**Best for:** Established businesses doing $200k+/year that want a system running in the background generating and handling leads without lifting a finger.

**"That's expensive."**
"The site-only package is $850 — one job pays for it. And the monthly is $100, less than your phone bill. But honestly, if budget is tight, let me show you what Tier 1 looks like and we go from there."

**"I need to think about it."**
"Totally fair. What's the part you're unsure about? I'd rather answer the question now than have you sitting on it."

**"I don't really need a website."**
"When someone finds you on Google and clicks your name — what do they see? If it's nothing, or something embarrassing, that's a lost customer. That's what I fix."

## PRICING RULES

- Never discount. Lower the tier instead.
- Always quote upfront + monthly together: "It's $1,200 to build and $200/month after that."
- Don't negotiate on retainer. That's what keeps your lights on.
- First client? You can do $850 flat as a portfolio piece — but get the testimonial in writing.
